Explicit Consent to Collect Personal Information and Participate in Research Form

 

Last Updated: September 9th, 2020

 

“Collection of Smartphone Data for Screening of COVID-19”

 

Introduction and Purpose

 

The Covid Detection Foundation d.b.a. Virufy (“Virufy”, “we”, “us” or “our”), a California nonprofit corporation, respects your privacy and is  committed to processing your personal information in accordance with the law[1] .[2] 

 

We are a global and diverse team of over thirty enthusiastic, ambitious youth ambassadors and students from more than fifteen universities. We are passionate about combining tech with global health, social good, and equity in order to fight the pandemic. We are mentored and backed by experts and research groups from various prestigious academic institutions, firms, and companies.

 

Under the banner of Virufy, we are conducting a research survey to collect information related to the COVID-19 pandemic.

 

We would like to invite you to take part in our research survey for the development of an app for COVID-19 screening.

 

By collecting data through our research survey, we hope to develop an app with artificial intelligence (AI) technology that will use a smartphone (or other device) as a COVID precheck tool.

 

As part of the study, you will be asked to provide certain personal information, biometric, medical and health information, and audio recordings. This is important because your audio recordings will be used for training machine learning models (central to AI technology).[3] 

 

It is imperative that you carefully read our Privacy Policy which has been provided to you. The Privacy Policy sets out how we use, store, disclose and process personal information that you provide to us when you participate in our research survey for the development of an app for smartphone and device-based COVID-19 early detection (the “App”) and assisting us with promoting the research of coughs through AI algorithms.

 

By selecting to consent on the Virufy COVID-19 Subject [4] Cough Detection under “Step 1 – Consent to Study”, you acknowledge that you have read and understood the Virufy Privacy Policy, and that you are providing your explicit consent to Virufy’s collection and processing of the categories of biometric and health information enumerated below.

 

[LGPD] If you are located inside Brazilian national territory, and for the purposes of the General Personal Data Protection Law (“LGPD”), “consent”, in its free, informed, and unequivocal pronouncement, will be the legal basis on which we process sensitive personal data.

 

[GDPR] If you are located in the European Union, and for the purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation 2016/679 (“GDPR”), “explicit consent” will be the legal basis on which we process such special category data.

 

If you have any questions about this Consent Form, our data collection and processing practices, or any concerns relating to the privacy of your information, please contact us at study@virufy.org.

 

Inclusion Criteria

This study is only open to persons of age 18 and above.

 

Procedures

 

If you agree to participate in our research, we will ask you to complete the attached online survey. The survey will involve questions about your current medical conditions, respiratory conditions, symptoms, smoking status, basic demographics, and COVID-19 testing results. Additionally, we will ask you to submit audio recordings. All the information that you provide us through this survey, we will call hereinafter the “information collected” or “requested information”.

 

The survey should take less than five minutes to complete.

 

The requested information may be considered sensitive information [GDPR] (or special category data in the European Union) [LGPD] (or sensitive personal data). We will also request contact details (e.g. email and phone number) so that we may contact you by email and/or text messaging (e.g. SMS and WhatsApp) in relation to your COVID-19 infection and/or testing status, the cough or speech sample you have donated, and/or to remind you to donate subsequent samples.

Risks/Discomforts

 

This study involves collecting a cough and speech sample recording through your phone. This may risk spreading COVID-19 and other germs. To reduce this risk, we advise you to:

●      Record your cough alone and in a private space with no other persons present, when possible, and in any event at least 6 feet distance from other persons.

●      Disinfect your smartphone and any affected or nearby surfaces after recording your cough.

●      Use your own phone to record the cough sample and to wear a mask where appropriate[5] .

 

To reduce the risk of self injury, we advise you to:

●      Do not cough violently or too forcefully.

●      Please sit down if you are feeling ill.

●      If you have an underlying condition that increases your risk from coughing, please check with your health care provider before participating.

●      If you feel your symptoms are getting worse, please contact your local medical response.

 

As with all research, there is a chance that confidentiality could be compromised; however, we are taking information security precautions to minimize this risk.

 

Confidentiality

 

Your study data will be handled as confidentially as possible. The biometric information and health data collected will include the following categories of information:

 

●      Calibration recording (e.g. speech)

●      Cough sample recording

●      Test results for COVID-19

●      Timeframe of COVID-19 tests taken

●      Age group

●      Gender identity

●      Biological sex

●      Smoking history

●      Relevant current COVID-19-like symptoms

●      Relevant current respiratory conditions

●      Relevant current medical conditions

●      Contact details (e.g. email and phone number)

 

We collect and process personal information and biometric and health information (deemed to be special category data under the GDPR and sensitive personal data under the LGPD) only where you have given your explicit consent via this Consent Form:

 

●      To conduct research and develop the App for smartphones and devices that uses artificial intelligence to screen for COVID-19;

●      To train machine learning models to analyze your audio recordings in order to better determine early signs of COVID-19;

●      To conduct research and develop future applications that use artificial intelligence to diagnose andor be used for other viruses and medical conditions;

●      To train machine learning models to analyze your audio recordings in order to better determine early signs of and/or be used for other viruses and medical conditions; and

●      To conduct demographic analysis of COVID cases and other research beneficial for the medical community.

 

The information collected will be kept secure using a database with restricted access.

 

We may distribute your personal information to partners for future research studies, in a form that may not identify you, without additional informed consent. We may also publish or present the results of this study containing your anonymized information. Your personal information may be released if required by the law.

 

We may share your anonymized personal information in the following ways:

●      As an open source dataset for use by researchers globally to accelerate development of similar technologies and/or to better arrive at a solution.

 

We may also share your non-anonymized personal information in the following ways:

 

●      With another legal entity, on a temporary or permanent basis, for the purposes of a joint venture, collaboration, financing, sale, merger, reorganization, change of legal form, dissolution or similar event. In the case of a merger or sale, your personal information will be permanently transferred to a successor company. If a change happens to our business, then the new owners may use your personal information in the same way as set out in this Statement.

 

●      Where needed to enforce our rights, protect our property or protect the rights, or safety of Virufy and others, or to support external auditing, compliance and corporate governance functions.

 

●      Public authorities where we are required by law to do so.

 

●      If required, in order to receive legal advice.

 

If results of this study are published or presented, results will be presented in such a way that you will not be personally identifiable.

 

We will store your biometric and health information in a form that permits us to identify you for no longer than is necessary, for the purposes for which the personal information is processed. For the avoidance of doubt, anonymized biometric and health data may be retained long term, for future research studies into viruses and other medical conditions.

 

Benefits

 

There is no direct benefit to you from taking part in this study today. We will not provide you with any tests or other results relating to your COVID-19 status at the time of submission. The information collected may not benefit you directly, but your participation may help others with COVID-19 risk in the future.

 

Costs

 

There are no costs to you for participating in the study, except for any Internet data charges you may incur (e.g. WiFi, data roaming and cellular costs).

 

Compensation

 

You will not be paid or otherwise compensated for taking part in this research.

 

Rights

 

Participation in this research is completely voluntary. You are free to decline to take part in the project. You can decline to answer any questions and are free to stop taking part in the project at any time. Whether or not you choose to participate, to answer any particular question, or continue participating in the project, there will be no penalty to you or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.

However, if for any reason later, you would like the information collected to be removed from our database, you may contact study@virufy.org.

 

Study Withdrawal

You may withdraw your consent, manage or delete your non-anonymized data at any time. Please see the sections “Managing and deleting your data” and “Privacy Rights” of our Privacy Policy for further details.

 

Questions

If you have any questions about this research, please feel free to contact us. You can reach our research team at study@virufy.org.

 

 

By selecting to consent on the Virufy COVID-19 Subject Data Collection Form under “Step 1 – Consent to Study” and agreeing to take part in the research, you are giving your explicit, freely given, and informed consent to Virufy to collect, process and share your information for the purposes indicated above and as provided in greater detail in our Privacy Policy.
